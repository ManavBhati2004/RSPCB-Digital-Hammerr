# Environment Day Campaign Certificate Design Prompt

## Objective
Create an ultra-premium, highly detailed, professionally designed **“Certificate of Participation”** for an **Environment Day Campaign** with a luxurious eco-friendly aesthetic. The certificate should appear official, elegant, modern, and suitable for government sustainability awareness initiatives.

The final output should feel like a professionally printed environmental recognition certificate with balanced typography, sophisticated ornamentation, and eco-conscious visual storytelling.

---

# Canvas & Layout

- Orientation: **Horizontal Landscape**
- Quality: **4K Ultra High Resolution**
- Style: **Flat vector + soft watercolor illustration**
- Composition: **Symmetrical and balanced**
- View: **Direct front-facing certificate**
- Background: **Cream / off-white textured premium paper**
- Texture: Subtle grain and watercolor shading
- No watermark
- No logos
- No mockup framing
- Print-ready aesthetics

---
